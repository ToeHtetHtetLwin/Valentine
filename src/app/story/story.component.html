The Origin

Chapter I

A Childhood Bound by Thread

My journey began with the rhythmic hum of my mother’s sewing machine—a sound that defined the soundtrack of my youth.

While others played, I was captivated by the transformation of raw silk. I spent countless hours transforming discarded remnants into miniature masterpieces. It was never just about clothing; it was about the architecture of grace.

"Fashion is the armor to survive reality. Every stitch should empower the wearer to tell their story without a single word."

Diana Atelier
